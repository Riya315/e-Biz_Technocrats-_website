Director’s Desk .

FOUNDER
Sanjeev Goyal, Director and Founder of the company has a career spanning 16 years of contribution in the field of Project Management, Software Quality Assurance and IT Trainings.He started his career with programming and gradually took up the responsibilities of design, analysis, team management, and project management. In the year 2000, he gave shape to his vision of emerging as an entrepreneur in IT sector by laying the foundation of e-Biz Technocrats Private Limited.
Mr. Goyal has played a leadership role in nurturing e-Biz Technocrats Private Limited and building it into a leading brand in entire North India. Under his leadership the organization successfully ventured into various divisions of IT and broadened its horizons.
FOUNDER
Vipul Goyal, has an MBA in Finance and Systems. He joined the organization in the year 2002. Mr. Vipul has played a pivotal role in shaping and expanding the working areas of e-Biz Technocrats Pvt. Ltd. Under his guidance, company has made many strategic alliances.
Along with taking care of responsibilities at e-Biz Technocrats Pvt. Ltd, he is also an Industrial Development Consultantunder MSME (An Indian Govt. Enterprise) and Branch Co-ordinator of Space Education & Training Academy, Gwalior, since 2007.
