Who We Are

Driven by Innovation, Defined by Quality

Established in 2003, e-Biz Technocrats Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 27001:2022 certified global IT service provider. Over the past two decades, we have partnered with businesses across the globe, delivering cutting-edge custom software development, mobile application design, cloud infrastructure, and marketing consultancy.

Our core strength lies in our collaborative culture, deep technical proficiency, and client-centric approach. We bring together developers, coordinators, and designers to architect robust digital solutions that scale operations, ensure transaction security, and power digital branding.

FOUNDER DIRECTOR

Sanjeev Goyal

Director & Founder

Sanjeev Goyal, Director and Founder of the company, has a career spanning 16+ years of contribution in the fields of Project Management, Software Quality Assurance, and IT Trainings. He started his career in software programming and gradually took up the responsibilities of systems design, analysis, team coordination, and project management.

In the year 2000, he gave shape to his vision of emerging as an entrepreneur in the IT sector by laying the foundation of e-Biz Technocrats Private Limited. Mr. Goyal has played a leadership role in nurturing e-Biz Technocrats Private Limited and building it into a leading brand in entire North India. Under his leadership, the organization successfully ventured into various divisions of IT and broadened its horizons.

FOUNDER CONSULTANT

Vipul Goyal

Founder & Leadership

Vipul Goyal holds an MBA in Finance and Systems and joined the organization in the year 2002. Mr. Vipul has played a pivotal role in shaping and expanding the working areas of e-Biz Technocrats Pvt. Ltd. Under his guidance, the company has forged many strategic alliances, helping deliver tailored ERP and CRM packages globally.

Along with taking care of responsibilities at e-Biz Technocrats Pvt. Ltd, he is also an Industrial Development Consultant under MSME (An Indian Govt. Enterprise) and has served as Branch Co-ordinator of the Space Education & Training Academy, Gwalior, since 2007, promoting aerospace training and educational platforms.
